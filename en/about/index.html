About

Andrey Gadzhibalaev (born July 29, 1992) is a Russian guitarist and composer living in the city of Magnitogorsk, Chelyabinsk Region. He is engaged in the creation of instrumental guitar music, combining different styles and genres.

Andrey began to study the instrument seriously at the age of 11, when he went to music school in the classical guitar study class. After a year, he got an electric guitar, and he began to master the playing technique on it, experimenting with hand positions and learning new techniques. His father, Igor Gadzhibalaev, who has been instilling a love for rock music into him since childhood, was a great influence.

Andrey wrote his first work at the age of 13 on a classical guitar, to which he gave the name “Sunset”. During these years, Andrey had a strong desire to learn how to make a self-made record. It all started with attempts to record an electric guitar through the Zoom 707 processor lineout, and then to the built-in Realtek AC’97 sound card using the outdated sequencer Cakewalk Music Creator 2002. Step by step, Andrew moved to the desired goal - to record a full track. It took many years to study many elements of sound recordings independently before making more or less satisfactory compositions in terms of quality.

In 2008, Andrey graduated from music school, being the winner of regional and international competitions in classical guitar. However, instead of further studies at the conservatory, he made a choice in favor of developing along an independent path and enters a technical university as a robotics engineer. Thus, music did not become his main profession, but remained the most sincere passion in life.

In 2009, he managed to record an album of 10 tracks called “One”. At the same time, auditions were held at the local guitar competition “Guitarshow”. 10 candidates were accepted, among whom was 17-year-old Andrey, who became a young participant. Under the terms of the competition, it was necessary for competitors to play 2 of their own compositions. It was the first time Andrew took to the stage with an electric guitar, in front of a large audience. He performed his compositions “Flame” and “Night Rain” from the album “One” written by him, made a strong impression on the public and the judges, which later recognized Andrey as the winner of the competition.

After this event, period of live concerts and participation in groups of various styles and genres came: alternative rock with the group “Part of Heat”, groove-metal with the group “Mad Punch”, progressive-metal project “Renovatio”, blues-funky project “Band of Exemplary Life” and fusion with the group of "L-Band". All this has become an invaluable experience for the development of improvisation and musical creativity.

After some time, Andrey decided to leave active work in groups and returned to his instrumental music. In 2014, the big two-disc album “Resurrection” was recorded, mixed and mastered independently at home and released after all. It also includes the compositions "Flame" and "Night Rain", re-recorded with improved quality. Three videos were shot for the tracks "Monolith", "Divergent" and "Moonlight Eyes".

Immediately after the release of “Resurrection”, Andrey started composing the new material. One by one, the compositions gradually began to take shape in the next album, and in the beginning of 2017, its release was planned. However, because of a negative set of circumstances, the hard disk with all materials and projects was damaged without the possibility of restoring and extracting useful information. Everything that was written over the long 3 years of continuous work turned out to be buried among the broken memory sectors.

«I clearly remember this moment... I only needed to adjust the equalization in the composition “Zerog”. All the other tracks were ready, and I already ordered a cover from my friend - Artem Mezentsev, who worked with me very well from my previous album and on the video shoot. But, when I started to open the project in Sonar X3, the grinding of hard disk mechanisms was the last sound I heard instead of my music. Scanning revealed the presence of more than 5,000 bad sectors in the first 10 gigabytes of total memory. I had to admit - it was all over ...»

After futile attempts at data recovery, it was decided to start everything from scratch: with a new PC and a new sound card, a complete rewriting of the material began.

«I felt it in my own skin and realized how much harder it is to rewrite what was done. You have mentally let go what you have done - you took it for the finished result. But at one moment nothing is left - a complete “erasure”. It was important not only to restore all the parts thoroughly, but the main thing was to re-breathe past emotions into the lost sounds» After a year and a half, in October 2018, the album “Erasing” was ready. It includes 11 tracks, still representing “salad styles”, but there are 4 bonus tracks, which are reworked versions of some tracks from the album “Resurrection”. At the moment, work is underway on the next album.
